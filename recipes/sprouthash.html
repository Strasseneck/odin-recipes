Sprout Hash

Description

A simple easy breakfast recipe.

The much maligned brussel sprout comes to life when paired with mushrooms and eggs in this one pan hash.

Ingredients

  • Brussel sprouts
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Two eggs

Steps

  1. Halve sprouts and discard thick outer skin
  2. Chop mushrooms into half and then half again and chop garlic
  3. Add sprouts to pan with hot oil
  4. When sprouts are browned on one side turn and add mushrooms with a knob of butter
  5. Stir in garlic
  6. Make two spaces in the pan for eggs and crack eggs into them being careful not to break yolk
  7. Cover with lid or baking paper until eggs are cooked through
  8. Serve
