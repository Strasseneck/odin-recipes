Dal

Description

Make a delicious smokey restaruant style tarka dal.

Using basic cheap ingredients to make something delicious.

Ingredients

  • Two cups lentils of choice
  • Thumb of ginger
  • Four cloves of garlic
  • Green chillis to taste
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Add two cups of lentils to pot with water and tumeric, chilli and ginger
  2. While dal is cooking prepare the tarka by chopping onions, garlic, tomatoes and combining the whole spices to toast
  3. When dal is cooked through, toast spices until they pop and become fragrant, add oil, onion and tomato and cook till onion is transparent
  4. When tarka is ready mix into dal.
  5. Serve.
